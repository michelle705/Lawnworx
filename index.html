Full-Service Lawn Care

At LawnWorx LLC, we handle every part of your yard — from the cut to the curb. Our crews provide precision mowing, clean weed eating and edging, mulch installation, lawn fertilizing, and brush clearing and hedge trimming for residential properties throughout East Pasco County.

We serve the communities of Zephyrhills, Dade City, San Antonio, Wesley Chapel, Mirada, and Epperson with a commitment to HOA compliance, razor-sharp edging, and consistent weekly scheduling. Your lawn is your home's first impression — we make sure it's a gold-standard one.

Florida's subtropical climate means your St. Augustine or Bahia grass grows fast during the summer rainy season — sometimes two to three inches in a single week. That's why dependable weekly service isn't a luxury in Pasco County, it's a necessity. LawnWorx shows up when we say we will, every time.

What We Do

Our Services

✂

Lawn Mowing

Weekly and bi-weekly mowing with sharp, commercial-grade blades. We cut to the correct height for Floratam St. Augustine and Bahia to keep roots healthy and prevent scalping.

🌿

Weed Eating

Precision string trimming and vertical edging along driveways, curbs, sidewalks, and fence lines. Every visit leaves crisp, clean lines that meet HOA standards.

🌳

Mulch Installation

Fresh mulch in garden beds retains moisture, controls weeds, and dramatically boosts curb appeal. We install, edge, and define beds for a clean, finished look.

🎯

Fertilizing

Properly timed fertilization keeps your Pasco County lawn green and dense. We apply the right nutrients for Florida's subtropical season — spring green-up through summer growth.

🚨

Brush Clearing

Overgrowth, invasive weeds, and brushy areas cleared efficiently. Essential for larger lots, rural properties, and new-construction homes in San Antonio and Dade City.

✂

Hedge Trimming

Hedges, shrubs, and ornamental plants shaped and trimmed to clean, geometric lines. We handle both routine maintenance and overgrown restoration cuts.
