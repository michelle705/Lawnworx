About LawnWorx

Serving Pasco County Since 2018

Built on Showing Up

LawnWorx LLC started in 2018 with a simple idea: show up on time, do the work right, and treat every yard like it matters. That idea hasn't changed. We've grown by earning the trust of homeowners across East Pasco County one property at a time.

We're a locally owned, licensed, and insured lawn care company serving Zephyrhills, Dade City, San Antonio, and Wesley Chapel. Our crews handle everything from weekly mowing and weed eating to mulch installation, lawn fertilizing, and brush clearing, and hedge trimming.

We specialize in HOA communities where consistency and standards matter — and we've maintained a 100% HOA compliance record across every property we service. When we leave your yard, every edge is sharp, every clipping is cleared, and every gate is secured.
